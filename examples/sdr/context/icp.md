# Ideal Customer Profile

> Replace this with your actual ICP. This is a template.

## Company

- **Size:** 50-500 employees
- **Stage:** Series A to Series C
- **Industry:** B2B SaaS
- **Signal:** Recently hired VP Engineering or Head of Platform

## Persona

- **Title:** VP Engineering, Head of Platform, CTO
- **Reports to:** CEO or CTO
- **Cares about:** Developer productivity, reducing context-switching, shipping faster

## Disqualifiers

- Pre-seed / bootstrapped (too early)
- Enterprise 1000+ (sales cycle too long)
- Non-technical buyer (can't evaluate the product)
