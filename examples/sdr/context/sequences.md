## Sequence: New VP Engineering Hire

**Trigger:** Company hired a new VP Eng in the last 60 days.

**Email 1:**
Subject: [Company] eng team

Body: Saw [Name] just joined as VP Eng — congrats. First 90 days usually means auditing the toolchain and figuring out where the team loses time.

We help eng teams at [similar company] cut context-switching by about 40%. Mostly by [one-line description of how].

Worth a 15-min look?

**Email 2 (3 days later):**
Subject: re: [Company] eng team

Body: Quick example — [Customer] had the same problem when [their VP Eng] joined last year. They were losing ~2 hours/dev/day to [specific problem]. After 3 weeks with us, they [specific result].

Happy to share exactly what they did.

**Email 3 (5 days later):**
Subject: close the loop?

Body: Don't want to be noise in your inbox. Should I stop reaching out, or is this worth 15 minutes?
