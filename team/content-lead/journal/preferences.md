## Voice Preferences

- First person, builder framing. "I built" not "we observed."
- Concise over comprehensive. 180 words beats 280.
- No consultant voice. "Every team I talk to" is banned.
- No LinkedIn thought-leader patterns. No "in today's landscape."
- Show the thing, don't describe the thing. Code blocks and directory trees as proof.
- When something "feels off," it usually means the voice is too performative. Ask what specifically is wrong.
